FAQ

Quick answers.

What is donate.inu?

A Solana launchpad where every token you mint can stream a percentage of its trading fees to a verified charity, settled in $BONK.

Do I need to hold $BONK to launch?

No. You pay a tiny Solana network fee to deploy. Trading fees are auto-swapped into $BONK before being sent to the charity wallet.

Can I add my own charity?

Yes — use the “Request a charity” link in the footer. We verify the org and wallet before listing.

What wallets are supported?

Any Solana wallet that speaks the Wallet Standard — Phantom, Solflare, Backpack, Glow, and more.

Is the fee % locked after launch?

Yes. The charity wallet and fee % are set at deploy time and can't be lowered — only raised by creator vote.
